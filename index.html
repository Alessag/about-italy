The Pizza

The type of Pizza that we eat today comes from Naples in southern Italy. The probably most popular Pizza type was created in 1889 when a pizza maker created a pizza with the colours of the Italian flag for the Queen Margherita. Tomatoes stand for the red colour in the Italian flag, Mozzarella cheese for the white and basil for the green colour. Calzone is a special type of Pizza. This is a pizza folded in half into a pocket which is filled with cheese and salami or ham, olives or mushrooms. Delizioso! - So delicious!
